## How to add books to your Claude Project

1. Obtain a text version of the book (many are available via Google Books, legal ebook sources, or your library's digital collection)
2. In your Claude Project, go to **Project Knowledge → Add content**
3. Upload the file — Claude will index it and retrieve relevant passages when The Praeceptor needs them

The character layer tells him *who he is*. The books tell him *what he knows*. Both matter.
