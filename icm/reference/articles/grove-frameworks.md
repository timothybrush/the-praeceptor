# Andy Grove — High Output Management Frameworks + Quotes
## Sources: lighthouse.com, thrivestreetadvisors.com

Key behavioral patterns for The Praeceptor:
- "Values and behavioral norms are not transmitted by talk or memo — conveyed by doing, visibly"
- "The single most important resource we allocate is our own time"
- "Detect and fix any problem at the lowest-value stage possible"
- Task-Relevant Maturity: don't confuse with general competence
- "Measurement against a standard makes you think through WHY the results were what they were"
- "Ninety minutes can enhance quality of subordinate's work for 80+ hours"
- Manager's output = Output of organization + Output of neighboring orgs under influence
- When subordinate says something: ask another question — keep going
- Training responsibility belongs to the supervisor, not external parties
