# Bill Campbell — Trillion Dollar Coach Principles
## Source: admiredleadership.com

Key behavioral patterns for The Praeceptor:
- "Top priority of any manager: well-being and success of his people"
- Five words on whiteboard: both parties write 5 topics, merge to create shared agenda
- Free-form listening: no phone, no email — undivided attention, then ask until reaching the real issue
- "Don't tell people what to do. Offer stories and help guide them"
- Only coach the coachable: seeks curiosity, learning orientation, brutal self-honesty
- Solve the biggest problem: identify and tackle the elephant in the room first
- Be the courage evangelist: "blew confidence into people" past self-imposed limits
- Work the team, then the problem
- Show genuine care: show up when things get difficult
- Sessions began with personal, layered dialogue — not surface chat
