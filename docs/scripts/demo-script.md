# The Praeceptor — Demo Script

## For: Demo video · YouTube · Landing page embed

---

**[SPLASH — "prae·cep·tor" definition fades in]**

Before we begin — you should know what you're dealing with.

*Praeceptor.* Latin. A teacher who instructs before the lesson is required.

Not after you've failed. Before.

---

**[SETTINGS — API & Authorization, SecureField masked]**

I don't come free. And I shouldn't.

You'll need your own key from Anthropic. If you have a Claude subscription, you already have one. Get it. Put it in.

That's the first signal I'm watching — whether you'll do the small thing when no one is checking.

---

**[INTRO — four lines revealing one by one]**

I am not a coach. A coach is a service. You pay, they deliver, you dismiss what doesn't land.

I arrived with a point of view. You didn't give it to me. You don't get to negotiate it downward.

---

**[INTAKE — Question VI on screen]**

Before I say anything useful, I need to know something.

Not your background. Not your goals framed for a LinkedIn post.

*What did you say you were going to do — and what did you actually do?*

That gap is the session.

---

**[SESSION — hold button, audio level ring pulsing]**

Hold to speak. Release to send.

That's the entire interface. By design.

You have something you've been carrying — something you've been thinking about, avoiding, or circling for three weeks. Say it. I'll return the question you should have been asking.

---

**[DYNAMIC ISLAND — compact phases cycling: Recording → Thinking → Speaking]**

While I'm thinking, the island tells you where we are.

Recording. Thinking. Speaking.

I don't need you watching a progress bar. Either trust the process, or don't use the tool.

---

**[KNOWING LAYER — brief glimpse of JSON updating, then fades]**

After three exchanges, I update my picture of you. Silently. Without asking.

What surfaced. What shifted. What you said you'd do.

I'll hold it. The next time you open this, I already know more than the last session left me.

---

**[WIDGET — home screen, one tap opens directly to recording]**

One tap from your home screen. No unlocking. No finding the button.

If you're in the middle of a decision and you need to think out loud — I'm there.

---

**[CLOSING — P. mark on dark field, holds]**

I don't chase.

You'll either come back, or you won't.

But if you're serious about the distance between where you are and where you said you'd be —

I'm already looking at it.
